Big Data Final Project

New York Center Coronavirus Data Analysis

Team Member: Zhi Zhang, Jessie Jin, Xuexuan Wang, Jenny Zhou

About this Project

As we are living in unprecedented times of pandemic, our team would like to put our efforts and contribute to battling COVID-19 health crisis. This project aims to help track and analyze the current COVID pandemic spread in NYC across the five boroughs: Manhattan, Queens, Brooklyn, and Staten Island. In addition, we included studies of relations between pandemic and its influential factors: age, income. Moreover, we analyzed impacted areas due to the burst of the virus: job market, business license application, public transportation, and permitted events. Based on current data, we forecasted the number of positive cases using machine learning algorithms.

  • Positive tests tracking map
  • Infections and fatalities analysis
  • Influential factors: Who is getting sick
  • Impacted areas: job market, license application, traffic collison and permitted events
